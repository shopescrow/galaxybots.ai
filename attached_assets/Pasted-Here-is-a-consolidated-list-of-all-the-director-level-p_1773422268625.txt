Here is a consolidated list of all the director-level positions mentioned, organized by category for easy reference.

1. Board of Directors (Governance)

· Chairperson (of the Board)
· Vice Chairperson
· Lead Independent Director
· Inside Director (e.g., CEO or Founder on the Board)
· Outside Director (Independent Director)

2. Corporate Directors (Management)

Executive Leadership (C-Suite Equivalents)

· Managing Director (often the highest executive role, especially outside the US)

Operations

· Director of Operations
· Plant Director / Director of Manufacturing
· Director of Supply Chain
· Director of Logistics
· Director of Procurement
· Director of Quality Assurance

Sales & Marketing

· Director of Sales
· Director of Marketing
· Director of Business Development (BD)
· Director of Communications (or PR Director)
· Director of Brand Strategy
· Director of Digital Marketing
· Director of Channel Sales / Partnerships

Finance & Legal

· Finance Director
· Director of Accounting (or Corporate Controller)
· Director of Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)
· Director of Legal Affairs (General Counsel)
· Director of Compliance
· Director of Risk Management
· Director of Internal Audit
· Director of Tax

Technology & Product

· Technical Director
· Director of Product Management
· Director of Engineering
· Director of IT (Information Technology)
· Director of Information Security (or CISO - Chief Information Security Officer)
· Director of Cloud Infrastructure
· Director of Data Science
· Director of Software Development

Human Resources

· HR Director (Human Resources)
· Director of Talent Acquisition
· Director of Learning & Development (L&D)
· Director of Total Rewards (Compensation & Benefits)
· Director of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)
· Director of Employee Relations

Creative & Design

· Creative Director
· Art Director
· Design Director
· UX/UI Director (User Experience/User Interface)

Specialized / Industry Specific

· Medical Director (Healthcare)
· Director of Clinical Operations (Pharma/Biotech)
· Director of Research (R&D)
· Director of Construction (Real Estate)
· Director of Facilities
· Director of Development (Non-Profit/Fundraising)
