## SLIDE 09 — Prospector — The Cloud 9 Score Engine

**Scene Cue:** Cloud 9 badge pulses in; five pipeline steps reveal in sequence

---

Prospector is our AEO Intelligence Engine — and it powers two things simultaneously: client value, and our own sales pipeline.

The Cloud 9 Score is our proprietary AI visibility index, scored from zero to one hundred, measured across nine AI platforms: ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Bing Copilot, Claude, Meta AI, DeepSeek, Grok, and Google AI.

For a client, the Cloud 9 Score answers a critical question every business should be asking: *"When someone asks an AI about my industry, does it mention me?"*

The five-step Prospector pipeline is: Discover — identify qualified prospects automatically. Enrich — add contact and company data. Qualify — confidence scoring with a human review queue. Contact — automated outreach sequences. Convert — CRM integration and deal tracking.

Beyond individual scores, Prospector tracks up to ten competitors' AI visibility in real-time — giving clients advance warning when they're losing ground before the market knows it.

The Human Review Queue is a deliberate design choice. Confidence scoring flags low-certainty records for human approval, because data quality is non-negotiable.

## SLIDE 16 — Legal & IP Strategy

**Scene Cue:** Four IP protection cards animate in 2x2; General Counsel Alexis quote panel appears

---

Our moat is not just technology. It is the legal architecture around that technology.

**Trademark Filings:** Cloud 9 Score™ and GalaxyBots™ are being trademarked in all target markets — U.S., UK, Canada, Australia, and EU in Year 4. This is not a formality. Owning the category metric name is a strategic asset.

**Trade Secrets:** Our bot personality frameworks and Institutional Memory architecture are protected as trade secrets — not patents. This is a deliberate choice. Patents require public disclosure of the method. Trade secrets protect the implementation while keeping the architecture confidential.

**Partner Contracts:** Every white-label partner operates under a robust reseller agreement that protects platform IP while giving partners the commercial flexibility they need. No partner can walk away and rebuild what we've built. That's by design.

**Compliance Roadmap:** SOC 2 Type II certification is targeted for Year 2. GDPR alignment in Year 4 ahead of EU expansion. Enterprise data governance is implemented from Day 1 — not retrofitted when a client demands it.

General Counsel Alexis makes the philosophy explicit: *"The moat is not just technology. It is the legal architecture around that technology. We build both simultaneously."*
